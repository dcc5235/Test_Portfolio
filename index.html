About

I am a former Higher-Ed Manager turned Front-End Developer. I am driven to find solutions that help people which is why I am passionate about user-friendly interface & accessibility for all.

I received a BA Administration of Justice with a minor in Sociology from the Pennsylvania State University, an MS Management with a concentration in Project Management from Colorado Technical University, and a certificate in Responsive Web Design from freeCodeCamp.

I love EDM & comedy. I enjoy continuously learning more adaptive approaches to responsive design, and collaborating with others to build stable and maintainable codebases. You can find me doing freelance work.
